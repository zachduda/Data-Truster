Facebook
F

With some of the most egregious privacy practices from selling user data without consent to Cambridge Analytica, to flat out lying about their data practices, Facebook has earned the lowest rank possible in terms of data trustworthiness. Using Facebook is the easiest way to sell out from any hopes of privacy. Recently, Facebook's parent company Meta has been caught lobbying to ban their competition for "data misuse" even though Facebook uses similar if not identical data practices.
